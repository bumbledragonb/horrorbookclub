Our Passion

The horror genre still has something of a reputation amongst the literary community. It is often seen as garish, or somehow disreputable - a crass art form that seeks thrills over quality. But fans of the genre know that this isn’t true. Horror remains one of the most popular genres around not only because of its visceral moments, but because, at its finest, it can explore the world in a way that no other stories can.

Horror uncovers our fears, and shows us the world as it really is. Horror Book Club exists so that our community can discover fantastic titles, both classic and new, together. Through our monthly reading selections, our book club discussions and our active Discord, we offer a place to explore the wider world of horror literature, and find your next moment of terror.

Our Mission

Horror Book Club is an inclusive community for fans of horror writing looking to explore their passion with others from all around the world. Our mission is simple - to champion the best in horror literature in a way that gives everyone a voice.

There are so many fantastic voices in the world, each with their own stories to share - and many of these are filled with the grotesque, the dark, the fearful and the monstrous. All the things we love most.

So Horror Book Club is a place where everyone, regardless of their background, has an equal say in shaping our reading, and discussing the books we lose ourselves in. We want to create a safe space to talk about literature’s most horrifying corners!
